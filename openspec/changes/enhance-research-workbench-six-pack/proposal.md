## Why

AstraLoom already connects papers, research ideas, and writing, but several workflows still feel like separate tools rather than one research system. Mature open-source projects point to six product patterns worth adopting: evidence-first answers, citation extraction/network views, project-structured LaTeX writing, STORM-style research planning, JabRef-style library quality controls, and Logseq-style backlinks.

## What Changes

- Add a PaperQA-style evidence confidence panel to paper AI answers.
- Add a GROBID-inspired citation network/readiness panel around paper detail and related papers.
- Add an Overleaf-style project file structure view to the writing manuscript workbench.
- Add a STORM-style research flow panel to research direction pages.
- Add JabRef-style citation key and metadata quality signals to the paper library.
- Add a lightweight cross-module knowledge graph view connecting papers, evidence, ideas, and writing.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `research-workbench-six-pack`: Cross-module research workflow enhancements spanning paper reading, paper library quality, idea generation, writing project structure, and linked knowledge navigation.

## Impact

- Frontend pages: paper detail, paper library, writing workbench, research project.
- New shared frontend components may be added for reusable graph/workflow panels.
